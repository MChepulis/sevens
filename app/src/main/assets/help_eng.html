Here you can know about how to play to Sevens.

  • Drag a hexagon block onto the honeycomb cells
  • Merge colors to reach a higher color
  • Tap on the hexagon to rotate them
  • Minimum color is Red
  • Maximum color is Violet
  • Use a hammer to remove a field element
  • Use a trash bin to kill the current hexagon
  • Try to score as many points as possible
  • The game ends, when the field is full
You ask, what is the order of the colors? It is simple - like in a rainbow:
Red, Orange, Yellow, Green, Blue, Indigo, Violet
